Project Description
To create a composition that included mouse interacton using any idea that I like .I chose Flocking since I love the marine life and flocking was something that I wanted to do.While Looking for an algorithm that had attactrive force and repel force ,I came accross the BOIDSalgorithm by Craig Reynolds.The basic flocking model consists of three simple steering behaviors which describe how an individual boid maneuvers based on the positions and velocities its nearby flockmates:
Separation
Alignment
Cohesion
